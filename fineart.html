Fine Art

My work consists of searching for an amalgam of drawn geological forms and the interior constructed architectural structure. A starting point is the forms of nature whereby by taking the shape of the mountainous shapes and abstracting the landscape to represent the essence of place and capture moments of time through colour. temporal aspects of the landscape is something I wish to bring out in the ambience of the piece looking at colour and line.
